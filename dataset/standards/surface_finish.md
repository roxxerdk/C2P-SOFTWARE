# Surface Finish and Roughness (Ra) Reference Standard

This standard provides values and inspection criteria for machined surface finishes.

## 1. Roughness Average (Ra) Values and Machining Limits
- **500 Ra (12.5 um)**: Very rough. Typical of raw casting, torch cutting, or deep slot roughing.
- **250 Ra (6.3 um)**: Standard rough cut. Sawing or fast rough milling.
- **125 Ra (3.2 um)**: General machining standard for most milled surfaces. Fully acceptable for structural brackets, housings, and non-sealing mounting surfaces.
- **63 Ra (1.6 um)**: High quality milled finish. Required for smooth sliding surfaces and gasket mating lines.
- **32 Ra (0.8 um)**: Precision surface finish. Requires fly cutting or slow finish pass. Ideal for O-ring sealing faces.
- **16 Ra (0.4 um)**: Extra-fine precision ground finish. Typically requires cylindrical or surface grinding.

## 2. Process Capability Chart
| Machining Operation | Normal Range (Ra) | Tight / Special Range (Ra) |
| ------------------- | ----------------- | -------------------------- |
| Sawing              | 125 - 500         | 63 - 1000                  |
| Rough Milling       | 125 - 250         | 63 - 500                   |
| Finish Milling      | 32 - 125          | 16 - 250                   |
| Drilling            | 63 - 125          | 32 - 250                   |
| Reaming             | 32 - 63           | 16 - 125                   |
| Grinding            | 8 - 32            | 4 - 63                     |
