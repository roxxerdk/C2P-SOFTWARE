# Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing (GD&T) Reference Standard

This document details inspection guidelines for GD&T features on machined components.

## 1. Primary Form Controls
- **Flatness**: The flatness tolerance limits surface variation between two parallel planes. Recommended tolerance limit: `0.002"` max per 6 inches of surface.
- **Straightness**: Typically applied to cylindrical shafts. Allowed deflection: `0.001"` per foot of shaft length.

## 2. Orientation Controls
- **Perpendicularity**: Limits tilt of a surface relative to a datum. Standard tolerance: `0.003"` relative to Datum A.
- **Parallelism**: Restricts slope relative to a datum plane. Standard tolerance: `0.004"` relative to base datum.

## 3. Location Controls
- **True Position**: Defines the allowable zone of deviation for holes relative to reference coordinate datums. General true position tolerance: `0.010"` at Maximum Material Condition (MMC).
